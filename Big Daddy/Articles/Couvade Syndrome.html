COUVADE SYNDROME

What do Arnold Schwarzenegger (circa 1994) and Couvade syndrome have in common? Apparently, men can get pregnant too. Kind of.

Although male pregnancy is still a little way off, some men who have a pregnant partner can suffer from actual pregnancy symptoms - from morning sickness, bloating, cravings and food aversions to mood swings, anxiety and poor concentration. This is Couvade syndrome.

We kid you not. And although evidence is limited, some studies suggest that Couvade syndrome could affect more than 1 in 10 dads-to-be. 

So next time (mother) eyes your belly and asks where her emergency stash of chocolate has gone, you now have an excuse - you could be nurturing your own sympathy pregnancy.
